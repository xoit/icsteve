Fireworks (2022/07/04)

Jul 5, 2022 1 min read fireworks independent

This year, we went to Shoreline Lake to view the Moutain View the independent day fireworks.

It is the first firework year after COVID. We are so close to the fireworks.

It is not a good experience to having the car park in the parking lot. Very difficult to go in and come out.

Never come near the “concert” and park the car in the parking lot.

Fireworks in Moutain View

At 9:45, the sky was almost dark, and the lights were off.

Closest to the fireworks

Since we went to the east side of the lake, we stopped at a place where people are gathered. We “guess"ed this would be a good place for watching the fireworks.

Indeed, this is the closet place to the fireworks. It looks like the fireworks shooters were just in front of us. This is the best reason why people are all here.

The sound is a little big but not so bad.

Back

This is a picture when we walk back to the parking lot.

Eventually, we got out and on the high way at around 10:38 pm.
