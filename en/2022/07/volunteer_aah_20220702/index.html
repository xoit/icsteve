Volunteer day at AAH (2022/07/02)

Jul 2, 2022 2 min read aah volunteer

Today is my 2nd time volunteer experience at AAH (https://www.animalassistedhappiness.org/).

Other than school volunteer, I have less experience on other organizations. AAH is providing the oportunities with the animals. I like close to the animals.

Clean the Horse House

We already know the process:

  • Pick and clean up the horse manure
  • Pile the leaves under the tree
  • Fill the water
  • Check and fill the big holes
  • Pick and dump the branches

This time, we have 5 people total.

I thought we only need 1 hour, actual we spent one and a half hours.

Me and my kid

My kid starts to take more resposibility of the volunteer work since last time, we were doing the same chores.

This time, my kid looks confident. My kid knows what to do and how to do. And we also checked with the Farm Manager that we should pick the tree branches.

I was trying to leave some work to my kid and let her go and communicate with others. We are together with 2 students, and the mother of one student.

She did a good job, first I feel she can handle the work and follow the Farm Manager’s instructions, of course we did one time before. And after almost all work is done, I asked her to talk to manager about our progress.

I did not capture that moment with photos, but I feel she is quite ready to talk to anyone who is more senior than her.

After talking to the manager, she asked me if she could go and carry the tank to pick up the tree branches. She did all by herself, including find a helper, pick up the keys for the dump area, carray the stuff and go.

My kid like to use the tool, when we carry some dirt from Bone Yard to the horse field, actually it is a little heavy for her. But she insist on doing it and when a elder student is trying to take away her oportunity to use the tool.

Eventually, it is really heavy. Three of us carried the “hand tipper”. I am not quite sure how to call that.

Visit favorite animal site

Once the horse cleaning is done, we goes to rabbit and guinea pig area to pet these small animals.

After pet the Guinea pig, which is the most popular place after the fram chores. We are back home.
