15. sometimes you want to combine several commands, there are several ways to do this. tcl is a good glue language, or other scripting languages, both of which are also convenient without compiling, and another option is to just use the csh alias command, other shells like that. Here comes the point, you can use "! *", "! :<num>" to access command parameters.

```
alias mycmd "echo \! * ;\\\
echo this is a long command \! :1 ; \\\
echo this is a very long command \! :2 ;"
```

17. Many times we use the "tee" command to write screen logs, multiple commands are not to use multiple tee and then cat together? No, tee also has append function, use "tee -a". 18.

18. Many times you have to use pipe + tee to write cshell scripts, it is very convenient to use ". /foo | tee foo.log", this only write out stdout, not srderr, need to debug when the error message in the foo.log is not, should be written as such

```
. /foo 2>&1 | tee foo.log
Or
. /foo |& tee foo.log
```

### 1. IC or CS

The first thing you need to do is to make sure that you have a good idea of what you want to do. If you are interested in IC, IC still have to do, as long as the industry does not disappear, there is still a demand for manpower; Speaking of CS is not more promising, my personal view is yes, the largest industry in the information age may be the software industry, but note that any industry positions have a bottom and top, CS so, IC is no exception.

### 2. Another reason to learn Tcl

A motivation to learn tcl: Many scripting languages support tk GUI programming, so by learning tcl/tk, you learn a GUI language, plus tcl is almost the most important Interface language for IC.

### 3. Copyright awareness

I see a lot of good sharing, but every time I see something, my first question is really, can this be used to spread? As my own notes may not be a problem, I gave up some expertise on the entry point to share, and instead share general knowledge, I see many articles to share is no problem, but need to pay attention to: how you protect the intellectual property rights of others today, tomorrow others will also respect your intellectual property rights.

### 4. CAD skill tree - PDK

CAD skill tree: master the structure of the PDK and the role of each file, and be familiar with how to use each file.

As a CAD engineer, you are often dealing with data, one of which is the PDK provided by foundry, called Process Design Kit, which is a modeling of a specific semiconductor manufacturing process, using the model provided in the PDK, together with the purchased EDA, you can design the chip, including but not limited to: technology file, design rule, spice... CAD skill tree: master the structure of PDK and the role of each file, and be familiar with how to use each file.

### 5. Timing Uncertainty (1 & 2)

Q: What is the purpose of setting timing uncertainty in chip design constraints? How do I set it?
Answer: When I first came into contact with constraints, I was doing Logical Synthesis and STA, at that time it was still 65nm, the constraints were not that complicated, and the experience value was good. After more and more exposure, from STA, APR to CAD, my thoughts are changing.

As an overview, uncertainty setting, as the name suggests, is used to cover the entire process of uncertainty, different stages need to consider different uncertainty, generally for the downstream work to reserve the timing space.

We are going to give examples from back to front, first STA timing signoff settings from the foundry process uncertainty, generally process manufacturers for the process line to do debugging, such as ocv. also include different products back to some of the experience of the actual test, the product on the yield, DFT test, work environment, aging and a series of product itself The product's own considerations.

Of course, STA has many other factors that may affect the uncertainty setting, such as the accuracy of STA, PBA vs GBA, the margin reserved for SI or transition, etc.

The next step is Routing, the more the previous step requires a certainty that includes the value of the subsequent steps plus some of the issues that need to be considered in this step, the routing uncertainty mainly covers the gap between global routing and real routing. EDA tools are constantly improving the accuracy of virtual routing and narrowing the gap between it and real routing. Specifically on more (not considering detour or congestion and other issues triggered by the large delay), to give an example of a little off, due to routing after the impact on the path delay more virtual will have longer or shorter, plus ocv, this value in several iteration can be estimated out, one of the empirical values.
