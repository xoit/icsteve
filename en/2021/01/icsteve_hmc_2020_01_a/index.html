CAD | Opinions

1. CAD and overtime

Overtime’s exist in any industry, construction workers build hospitals in a few days, software Release phase overtime, hardware Tapeout final to sprint. If things could be parallel, wouldn’t there be no need for overtime? Not really. When things are parallel, the cost of resources consumed for the same goal is increased, and under certain conditions (overtime), the cost is lowest. The goal of ICCAD is to increase efficiency, and in some cases with the help of CAD, touch the fish and reduce the intensity of your own work. ICCAD also has the advantage that CAD can dabble in more areas, strengthening its moat and enhancing its indispensability.

2. Using APIs is a basic CAD skill

No matter what framework or system is becoming more and more closed, and with open source systems like Android, users get a hundred flowers, each with good and bad effects. So what can CAD cope with? The data interface will become more and more open, and the ability to access data is a basic skill of CAD.

3. What ICSteve will share

There are many ways to share and export the summary knowledge of CAD and IC alone. After years of working with CAD, I have found that there are several types of capabilities that can significantly improve a designer’s productivity and experience.

Continuous optimization of the development environment Modularization of basic knowledge, instantiation, data, and tools

Knowledge is a treasure but practice is the key

4. CAD and Software Development

If you want to take CAD further, my suggestion is to do both upward and downward management, going upward through the architecture and participating in CAD decisions at the management level, and going downward to understand the organization and bottlenecks of hardware projects and provide solutions.

5. Chip design in the cloud

The key is the word “save”, either save time, shorten Time to Market, or save cost, reduce the cost increase brought by peak usage. After the cloud, the right to use the data, whether your design will become a sample to be learned and other issues need to be considered. On the cloud may be some positions will be reduced, there are also some positions will be added, such as cloud data interaction (learn some container technology, network technology always overflow).
