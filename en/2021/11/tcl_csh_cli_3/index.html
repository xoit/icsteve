Project technology selection (tcl-csh-cli)

Nov 24, 2021 1 min read CLI Csh PM Tcl

In this post, I would like to talk about my view on technology selection.

When we talk about VLSI CAD (not EDA), most people will think about CS background, coding ability, etc.

But I want to think out of box, as a project manager, I can not just pick option 1 or option 2. It is always good to use SME (Subject Matter Expert) tool. So we may need to group a team with option 1 and option 2 mixture.

  • Option 1: Gather a group of software developers
  • Option 2: Have the domain experts, ASIC design experts

My option is that, it is better to have some domain specific knowledge before doing software development. Like if we don’t know the area of our client who is in finacial industry, maybe it is not easy to communicate and maybe the software is good but more like a generic one, not designed for our customer.
